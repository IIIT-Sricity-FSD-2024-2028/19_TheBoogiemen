The Problem

Most students fail
silently.

By the time a faculty member notices a student is in trouble — attendance has slipped, assignments pile up, scores tank — it’s often too late in the semester to do anything about it.

BarelyPassing surfaces risk early, connects the right people, and gives academic heads the data they need to act — not react.
